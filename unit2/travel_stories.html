Eiffel Tower - Paris

The Eiffel Tower is one of the most famous landmarks in the world. It is located in Paris, France, and attracts millions of visitors every year. The tower offers stunning views of the city and is a symbol of French culture and architecture.

Colosseum - Rome

The Colosseum is a historic amphitheater in Rome, Italy. It was used in ancient times for gladiator contests and public events. Today, it remains one of the most important examples of Roman engineering and attracts visitors from around the globe.

Statue of Liberty - New York

The Statue of Liberty is a major symbol of freedom and democracy in the United States. It stands on Liberty Island in New York Harbor and was a gift from France. It is one of the most recognized monuments in the world.
